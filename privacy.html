Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Ballew Parfum ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit or make a purchase from our website.

Information We Collect

When you place an order or interact with our website, we may collect certain personal information including your name, email address, shipping address, and payment details necessary to process your order.

Payment information is processed securely through Stripe. We do not store or have direct access to your full credit card details.

Email Communications

If you subscribe to our mailing list or join the Ballew Parfum Club, we may collect your email address to send updates, promotional offers, product announcements, and other communications related to our brand.

Email communications are managed through EmailOctopus. You may unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time using the unsubscribe link included in our emails.

Cookies

Our website may use basic cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience, maintain website functionality, and support payment processing sessions.

These cookies do not collect sensitive personal information and are used only for basic website functionality.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Process and fulfill orders
  • Provide customer support
  • Send updates about products or promotions
  • Improve the functionality and performance of our website

California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have certain rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA). These rights may include the ability to request access to the personal information we collect about you or request that we delete your information.

To make a privacy-related request, please contact us using the information below.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
