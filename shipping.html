Shipping & Returns

Shipping

Ballew Parfum currently ships within the United States only.

Orders are typically processed and shipped within 2–5 business days. Processing times may vary during high order volume or limited product releases.

Shipping is provided via USPS Ground or a comparable carrier. We reserve the right to select the most appropriate carrier for your order.

Shipping within the United States is offered at a $10 flat rate.

Because fragrances contain alcohol, they are classified as flammable goods and may ship using ground transportation in accordance with carrier safety regulations.

Returns

Due to the nature of fragrance products, we cannot accept returns on opened bottles.

Unopened products may be returned within 30 days of delivery for a refund. Returned items must be unused, unopened, and in their original packaging.

Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item received was incorrect or damaged.

Damaged or Incorrect Orders

If your order arrives damaged or if you receive the wrong item, please contact us within 7 days of delivery.

Please include your order number and photos of the issue when contacting us so we can resolve the matter as quickly as possible.
